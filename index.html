Sushila Birla Girls' School

ABOUT THE SCHOOL Sushila Birla Girls' School provides a warm, caring, stimulating and challenging environment in which our students learn and develop. We aim to provide an education which explores and strengthens the potential which is innate in every individual but awaiting expression. In order to do so, we seek to provide an environment which enriches the intellectual, spiritual, emotional and physical development of the pupil in an atmosphere that is happy and nurturing. Emphasis is thus placed on promoting personal achievement, building a sense of responsibility and ultimately securing a strong sense of self.

International Dimensions In School Award

Sushila Birla Girls‚Äô School has been awarded the coveted International Dimension in Schools Award (formerly, International School Award) conducted under the aegis of the British Council for the three year period 2020-23. The dossier submitted under the scheme obtained the maximum possible score of 56. The British Council has commended the school for the work done in various projects. They have praised them as excellent examples of collaborative work done with an international dimension by the students and the teachers with their counterparts in different countries. These projects showcased the extensive research done by the students and the teachers both in India and overseas.



5th International Karate Championship 2019

July The 5th International Karate Championship 2019 was held on 26th, 27th and 28th July at Netaji Indoor Stadium. There were 4000 participants from countries like India, Bangladesh, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Bhutan, Iran etc. The championship is organised every year by Hanshi Premjit Sen. It is because of his efforts that Indian students get an international platform to showcase their talents. Rupkatha Chatterjee of class II A participated in two individual events - Kata and Kumite, She won a gold medal in Kata and a silver in Kumite.
